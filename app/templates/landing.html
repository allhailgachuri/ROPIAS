Traditional farming calendars told Kenyan farmers:

Plant when the Long Rains begin in March.

Climate change broke that calendar.

ROPIAS gives it back.

False onsets — brief early rains followed by 14-21 day dry spells — now cost Kenyan smallholder farmers their entire seasonal investment in seeds, fertiliser, and labour. ROPIAS detects the difference before the seed goes in the ground.

STEP 01

Enter your farm's GPS coordinates

Or tap 'Use My Location' on your phone. ROPIAS works for any location within Kenya's agricultural zones.

📍 0.2800°N, 34.7500°E
STEP 02

NASA reads the sky

60 days of corrected daily precipitation (PRECTOTCORR) and root zone soil wetness (GWETROOT) from NASA's POWER API — no physical sensors, no equipment to install.

Plain-language advisory delivered instantly
